Places to visit
    Diamond head is and old dormant volcano and one of the most iconic tourist locations in Honolulu Hawaii.
    Hanauma bay is one of the favorite beaches in all of hawaii showcasing green sea turtles and parrot fish.
    Koko head is a great area for the outdoorsman in all of us, visitors can mountanier and climb a dormant volcano
    Pearl harbor is an old WWII memorial that is a great place for people learn about a pivotal event in American history
