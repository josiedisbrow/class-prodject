About
Honolulu, on the island of Oahu’s south shore, is capital of Hawaii. In this city you’ll find everything from historic landmarks, fine dining and world-class shopping. Honolulu stretches across the southeastern shores of the island,from Pearl Harbor to Makapuu Point.
